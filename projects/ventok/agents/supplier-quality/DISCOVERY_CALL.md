# Discovery Call Script — Supplier Quality Agent

**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Goal:** Validate pain, understand budget, move to POC

---

## Opening (1 min)

**"Hi [name], thanks for the call. We help manufacturers avoid supply chain disasters. I've got 3 quick questions to understand if it's a fit. Cool?"**

*(Assumes you already have initial interest — cold outreach will be in email first)*

---

## Discovery (12 min)

### Q1: Current State (3 min)
**"Let's start simple: how many suppliers do you work with, and which countries?"**

*Listen for:*
- 5+ suppliers → good pain potential
- International (Asia, Russia, EU) → language barriers → high pain
- Examples: "We work with 12 suppliers across 4 countries — Poland, Vietnam, Germany..."

**Follow-up:** "And how many of those are international?"

---

### Q2: Current Process (3 min)
**"How do you currently track supplier status right now? Are you monitoring emails, spreadsheets, calls...?"**

*Listen for:*
- Manual email monitoring → pain signal
- Spreadsheets with manual updates → pain signal
- WhatsApp + phone calls → high friction signal
- No centralized system → red flag / opportunity

**You want to hear:** "We mostly just monitor emails, but it's chaotic" or "We use WhatsApp but it's scattered"

---

### Q3: Cost of Failure (3 min)
**"What was your worst supplier issue last year? How much did it cost you?"**

*This unlocks the real pain:*
- Defects shipped to customer → recall → reputation hit = €50-100k
- Delivery missed → production halted → customer angry = €20-50k
- Quality drop discovered after shipment = €30-80k
- Wrong goods shipped = €10-30k

**If they say "I don't know":** "Ballpark — if a batch was bad and you had to rework it, what's that worth?"

**Follow-up:** "How often does something like that happen?"

*You want 2-3 issues/year for good ROI story*

---

## Pitch (2 min)

**"So here's what we see: you have suppliers across languages and timezones. Issues get discovered after they're expensive. You want better visibility earlier."**

**"We built an AI agent that reads your supplier emails 24/7, catches patterns humans miss, predicts problems before they ship."**

**"Instead of spending 15 hours/week on supplier email triage, your team focuses on relationships and strategy. Cost is €1.5k/month."**

**"The thing is, we want to prove this works for YOUR specific suppliers first. So here's what I'm proposing:"**

---

## Proof of Concept Offer (1 min)

**"We take your last 100-150 supplier emails. Run them through our agent. Show you what it would have caught, what patterns it sees, what it missed. No charge. Takes us 1 week."**

**"If you like what you see, we build the full agent. If you don't, no cost, no obligation."**

**"Fair?"**

---

## Closing Questions

**If they say YES to POC:**
- "Great. Can you send us a sample of supplier emails? Just the last month or two, via email."
- "I'll have results to you by [date + 1 week]."
- "Then we can talk next steps."

**If they say "Let me think about it":**
- "Totally fair. What questions do you have?"
- "What would make this a yes for you?"
- "When's a good time to follow up — end of week?"

**If they say NO:**
- "No worries. What's the concern?"
- *Listen* — might be budget, timing, trust, etc.
- "Would it be different if [address concern]?"

---

## Key Points to Remember

### DO:
- ✓ Ask questions, listen more than you talk
- ✓ Focus on THEIR pain (not our tech)
- ✓ Mention cost early (€1.5k/mo) — screens out tire-kickers
- ✓ Offer POC as risk-free trial
- ✓ Get email sample before ending call

### DON'T:
- ✗ Over-explain Claude / AI / how it works
- ✗ Promise perfection (agents aren't robots)
- ✗ Leave without next step (get email sample or follow-up date)
- ✗ Talk about features — talk about outcomes (fewer issues, more time)

---

## Follow-Up (After POC)

**Email template (if they want to see it):**

```

I ran your last [100] supplier emails through our agent. Here's what jumped out:

PATTERNS WE CAUGHT:
- Supplier A historically misses deadlines by 3-4 days; they did again here
- Supplier B had quality variance; we see similar flags as in [previous incident]
- 3 emails you received had missing PO numbers (hidden risk)

WHAT WE'D CATCH GOING FORWARD:
- Delivery risk: [Supplier C] mentioned "slight delay" — would flag for your attention
- Quality drift: [Supplier A] defect rate trending up 1-2% per batch (saw this before)
- Communication gap: [email example] was missing critical detail; agent would ask for clarity

NEXT STEP:
If this resonates, let's build the real thing. Full agent goes live in 4 weeks.
Cost: €1.5k/month, or €8k one-time build.

Questions? Call me.

[Your name]
```
