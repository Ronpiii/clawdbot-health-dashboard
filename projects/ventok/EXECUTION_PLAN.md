**Target:** 4-5 customers @ €1.5-2k each

**Mechanics:**
- 1 customer conversation/week = 40 conversations/9 months
- 5-10 discovery calls per customer (accounting for no-shows, rejections)

- Only need 3-4 paying to hit €5k/mo
