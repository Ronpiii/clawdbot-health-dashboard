### ⚠️ critical finding: overfitting
walk-forward validation (70/30 split):
- in-sample: EV +11.1%, WR 66.7%
- out-sample: EV -2.7%, WR 50.0%
- **degradation: -124%** — strategies that look great historically may not work forward

### honest conclusion
