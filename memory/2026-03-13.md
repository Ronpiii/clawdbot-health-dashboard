## lessons learned

- **market conditions matter**: 24h backtest window was sideways/choppy (baseline lost -$5.77). longer windows needed for validation.
- **parameter sensitivity**: EMA period strongly affects trade count/quality. 140 < 200 on current data.
- **tighter stops work**: competitors beat baseline by tightening SL from 2% → 1% + adjusting targets (risk/reward shift).
- **variant approach works**: 35% of random samples beat baseline despite small window. larger search space likely finds stronger configs.
