## BTC Trading System — Built & Live

### Strategy Decided: Regime-Filtered EMA
- **filter:** 200-day EMA determines regime (bull/bear/chop)
- **signal:** 20-day EMA determines entry/exit
- **rules:**
  - price > 200 EMA = BULL → longs only
  - price < 200 EMA = BEAR → shorts only
  - price ±3% of 200 EMA = CHOP → half size or skip
- **leverage:** 3x
- **risk:** 2% per trade
- **stop:** 6% from entry
