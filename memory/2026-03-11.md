Ron shared a trading config with 10% win rate. **Critical issues identified:**

1. **10% win rate = whipsaw problem**
   - Price touching EMA 200 and immediately bouncing back (no follow-through)
   - Slope threshold (0.02) too loose; catching reversals not entries

2. **Missing 4H trend filter**
   - Reactive EMA alone = every oscillation becomes a trade
   - Add: Only BUY if 4H EMA rising, only SHORT if 4H EMA falling
   - This kills 70% of false entries alone

3. **No volatility gate**
   - Low-vol choppy markets are poison
   - Add: Skip trades if ATR < 50 USD or Bollinger Band width below 20th percentile
   - Or: Skip during London-US overlap (11:00-14:00 UTC) when spreads widen

4. **Entry precision broken**
   - Entering at EMA *touch*, should enter at EMA *break* (0.1-0.2% beyond)
   - Needs 2-candle confirmation: price closes beyond EMA for 2 consecutive candles

5. **Fee/slippage not accounted for**
   - Hyperliquid: 0.05% taker fee entry + 0.05% exit = 0.1% total
   - At 10% win rate + 0.1% fees, strategy is underwater on cost alone
   - Backtest must include: taker fees, market impact on LIMIT orders, liquidation buffer (keep 5-10% margin)

6. **Sample size too small**
   - 10 trades = noise. Need 50+ for validation
