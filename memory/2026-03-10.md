## Trading Bot Backtest Work (Evening)

### Goal
Validate 200 EMA 5-minute strategy with real Binance data.

### Key Findings

**200 EMA Crossover (no stoploss) on 5m:**
- **30 days:** 150 trades, 17.3% win rate, **+3.29% total**
- Structure: many small losses (124) + few big winners (26)
- Example winners: +4.3%, +6%, +5.7%, +3.1% (each worth 5-15 small losses)
- Max drawdown: 6.84%

**200 EMA with stoploss (tested 1%-10%):**
- All levels identical: 150 trades, 17.3%, +3.29%
- **Why:** Exits happen on reverse crossovers, NOT stoploss hits
- Stoploss level doesn't matter (price reverses before hitting it)

**200 EMA with fixed 5% stoploss:**
- **7 days:** 1 trade, +2.58%
- **30 days:** 1 trade, +0.45%
- Strategy is too slow (EMA200 changes glacially on 5m)
- Only catches major trend reversals

### Critical Insight
Crossover-only approach is **more profitable** (+3.29%) than fixed-stoploss approach (+0.45%) because:
1. Crossovers naturally exit positions when trend reverses
2. Whipsaws are small losses
3. Real trends produce asymmetric wins (big gains on reversals catch half the move)
4. Net result: low win rate but positive expectancy

**Option B: 1 trade, +0.45%** (fixed SL, clean but misses moves)
