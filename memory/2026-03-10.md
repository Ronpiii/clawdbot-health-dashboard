**Position Scaling Implemented: +50% at +2% Profit**
- adds second tranche when position +2% in profit
- caps at 2 tranches max
- backtest result: +70% PnL improvement on synthetic data (BTC 120k → 205k)

**4H Candle Close Confirmation for Entries**
- prevents retests/false breakouts — waits for 4H close to confirm
- signal triggers → pending state → wait 4H candle close → check price stayed above/below EMA
- if retest: rejected (price bounced back)
- if confirmed: executes entry at current price
- existing positions unaffected; only applies to new entries

- positions-card recalculates margin with 5x multiplier
- margin utilization now 62% (was higher with 3x sizing)

## lessons learned

1. **EMA responsiveness tradeoff:** EMA50 too noisy (139 regime flips in 3mo), EMA100 sweet spot (94 flips), EMA200 lagged (44 flips but caught fewer trades). go EMA100 for regime gates.

2. **4H confirmation prevents false entries:** touching EMA ≠ conviction. waiting for close filters retests without killing overall win rate. entry quality > entry frequency.

3. **Scaling into winners compounds:** +70% backtested improvement suggests partial position entry then pyramiding is vastly better than all-in on signal. 50% + 50% two-tranche cap prevents over-leverage.

4. **Discord alerts keep visibility:** live trading benefits from instant trade notifications in shared channel. positions card + regime changes + scaling events all posted = transparency for monitoring.

5. **Leverage management matters:** 5x vs 3x only meaningful with proper position sizing. 62% margin utilization leaves room for scaling, drawdowns, surprise entries. don't max out margin early.
